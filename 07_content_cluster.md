## Publishing Order

Publish in this sequence for maximum SEO impact:

### Step 1 — Publish the Pillar Blog First

**Why:** The pillar blog establishes the core keyword on your domain. All subsequent supporting blogs will link back to it — so it needs to exist first. Publishing it first also gives Google time to index it and begin evaluating its topical relevance.

**Action after publishing:**
- Share on WhatsApp broadcast to former students and leads
- Post a summary on the institute's Facebook and Instagram pages
- Submit URL to Google Search Console for indexing

---

### Step 2 — Publish Blog 1: How to Improve Spoken English Confidence

**Why:** This is the highest-traffic awareness blog. It targets a broadly searched informational query and will attract readers who are not yet ready to enroll but are building familiarity with the topic — and with the website. It links back to the pillar, passing early authority.

**Action after publishing:**
- Share in local student Facebook groups and WhatsApp communities
- Pin to Instagram Stories with a link in bio

---

### Step 3 — Publish Blog 2: Best Spoken English Course for Job Seekers

**Why:** Job seekers are a high-intent audience. This blog moves readers from general interest into active comparison — and the CTA directly invites them to visit or call. The internal link to the interview tips blog (Blog 3) sets up the next step in the cluster.

**Action after publishing:**
- Share in Chennai job seeker and placement groups on LinkedIn
- Post a carousel version of the "common interview mistakes" section on Instagram

---

### Step 4 — Publish Blog 3: Interview English Tips for Freshers

**Why:** This blog targets one of the highest-intent secondary keywords in the cluster. Freshers preparing for placement season are actively looking for exactly this kind of content. Publishing it after the job seeker blog means Blog 2 can already link to Blog 3 with a live URL.

**Action after publishing:**
- Share during placement season (October–February) for maximum impact
- Use "5 interview English tips for freshers" as a WhatsApp broadcast series

---

### Step 5 — Publish Blog 4: Why Personality Development Matters for Career Growth

**Why:** This blog broadens the topical footprint of the cluster beyond "spoken English" into the adjacent territory of professional development — allowing the website to rank for a new set of keywords while reinforcing the authority built by the previous four pieces.

**Action after publishing:**
- Share with working professionals via LinkedIn post
- Frame it as "career content" rather than "English class marketing" for wider reach

## Content Cluster Health Checklist

Use this checklist when the full cluster is live:

- [ ] All five pieces are published and indexed
- [ ] Pillar blog links to all four supporting blogs with editorial anchor text
- [ ] All four supporting blogs link back to the pillar with relevant anchor text
- [ ] Supporting blogs cross-link where topically relevant
- [ ] All pages have a unique meta title and meta description with the target keyword
- [ ] All pages have a clear CTA (call/visit/demo class)
- [ ] No two pages target the same primary keyword (no cannibalization)
- [ ] Google Business Profile is updated with website links
- [ ] Blog URLs submitted to Google Search Console
